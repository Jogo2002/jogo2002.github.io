About bananas

Bananas are a soft, sweet fruit that are easy to eat on the go. This page follows the same structure as the rest of the site for consistent formatting.

They’re often used in smoothies, cereal, and desserts like banana bread. Because they’re naturally sweet, they’re also common in snacks.
